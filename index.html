NSITSoc

The hub of all Societies

Forgot about the event?

Many of us want to attend orientation/events/SIGs of different societies of NSIT but are often confused when is the event? or where is it?
The information shared on facebook and whatsapp by the societies is often lost due to infinite memes on our fb news feed and unlimited spam messages on whatsapp !

So Whats the Solution ??

NSITSoc brings all the recent events, orientations, SIGs, meetings of various societies of NSIT all in one place. Just download the app, sign-in and you are ready to go !!

Some Features of NSITSoc:-

  • -> Simple user interface for easy use and navigation
  • -> Compatible with all devices above android 4.0
  • -> Small in size, eats up less space in your phone
  • -> Tumblr like Redux layout
  • -> Google Maps API to pin the location of the location of the event
  • -> Option for societies to put other social media links on their groups
  • -> Option for pinning important posts at the top.
  • -> Save the event to your google calender and get a reminder later
  • -> Background Updates whenever device connected to the internet
  • -> Custom Notifications for particular societies
  • -> Share the event directly to your Whatsapp/fb pms or copy a shareable link
  • -> Private posts for admins of various Societies
